Stay Safe Online – Checklist 

# General Safety Tips

✔ Use strong & unique passwords

✔ Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

✔ Do not click on unknown links

✔ Be careful with public Wi-Fi

✔ Limit personal information online

✔ Download apps only from trusted sources

✔ Regularly update devices and apps

✔ Verify before trusting (calls/messages)




#UPI & Financial Safety (VERY IMPORTANT)

✔ Never share OTP or UPI PIN

✔ “Receive money” ≠ entering PIN

✔ Check QR codes before scanning

✔ Use trusted apps only



## If Something Goes Wrong

✔ Immediately report fraud
Report any cybercrime at:
 [https://www.cybercrime.gov.in]
 Helpline: 1930

✔ Act quickly for financial fraud
The faster you report, the higher the chances of recovering your money.

 Final Tip

✔ **Stay alert, not scared
Most scams succeed due to panic or urgency—always pause and verify before taking action.
